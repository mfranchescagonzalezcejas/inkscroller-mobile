## What's new in v0.6.0

### Personal Library
Save manga to your library and track your reading status — Added, Reading, Completed, or Paused. Your library syncs to your account automatically.

### Reading Progress
The app remembers where you left off in every chapter. Progress is saved locally and synced across sessions.

### Reader Settings Panel
Adjust settings without leaving the reader:
- Reading direction (vertical scroll or paged)
- Screen brightness
- AMOLED black mode
- Immersive mode (hides system UI)

### New Auth Experience
Completely redesigned login and register screens.

### Performance improvements
- Cover images preload when you open the home screen
- Library grid loads progressively as you scroll
- Background sync indicator while your library updates

---

**Requirements:** Android 5.0+ · Enable "Install from unknown sources" before installing.
